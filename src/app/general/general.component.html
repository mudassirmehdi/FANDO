¡Bienvenidos a FUTBOL PUNTO SHOW CHALLENGE! Esta competencia incluye Fase de Grupos, así como Fase de Eliminatoria, lo que significa que debes de llenar dos hojas de selecciones. La Fase de Grupos incluye el ranking de como los 32 equipos van a quedar en cada grupo y debe ser completado antes del 20 de noviembre. Luego, al pasar a la Fase de Eliminatoria, la segunda selección se realizará donde se seleccionará juego por juego con los 16 equipos de esta fase. Tu puntuación será determinada en base a la sumatoria de ambas Fases. Buena suerte a todos los #Showseros. Si tienen preguntas, pueden escribirle a mundial@futbol.show.

Regard de pool
  • Each participant is limited to a single entry in the contest.
  • Members will submit two sets of picks.
    • Before the tournament starts, members will submit their picks for the group stage. For this pick sheet, members will guess the finishing position for each country in each group.
    • When the group stage is over, members will submit their picks for the knockout stage in a standard bracket pick sheet, picking the winner for each game.
  • The pick deadline for the group stage will be when the first game starts, and is currently set to November 20, 2022 at 11:00 AM ET.
  • Members will receive the following points for each correct group finishing position: First:1 pt. Second:1 pt. Third:1 pt. Fourth:1 pt.
  • Members will receive the following points for each correct pick in the knockout stage: Round of 16:1 pt. Quarter Final:2 pts. Semi Final:4 pts. Final:8 pts.
  • The tie-breaker is the total number of goals in all 15 games of the knockout round, excluding shootout goals.
